Ang jowa kong crosswise

by Tsambolero

Ang Jowa Kong Crosswise chronicles the life and times of Tomas and Leo, who are, for all intents and purposes, an unremarkably average couple in their mid-twenties... or so it would seem.

Tomas is actually a self-segmenting winged creature of the night, while Leo is, well, just Leo.

Take an intimate peek into the mundanities of their relationship and watch as this extraordinary pair go through their very ordinary lives.
